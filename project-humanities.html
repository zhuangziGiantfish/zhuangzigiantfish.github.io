Humanities — Zhuangzi & Tiantai

Seminar work at UChicago Divinity School with Prof. Brook Ziporyn; translation/interpretation of classical Chinese texts (Zhuangzi; Tiantai).

Minimal note

Work included reading and discussion of Zhuangzi and Tiantai Buddhist texts, with emphasis on translation choices and philosophical interpretation. In seminar, we discussed draft ideas related to the complete translation of Zhuangzi.
